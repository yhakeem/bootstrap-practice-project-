Generation Skate Was Founded in 2022 by 4 Web Developers who also had a passion for skating. At Generation Skate we believe in adventure, determination, and living life on your own terms. All proceeds are donated to Generation USA a nonprofit technical accelerator program helping people transition into the technical field. In addition to selling our uniquely designed boards, we also have a board donation program where people can donate lightly used and refurbished boards to others. Take a look at our site and don't forget to live wild!
